PHASE_01: CLINICAL_LOCK

VERIFICATION & HYGIENE

Dawa lagane se pehle hamara nursing staff doctor ki prescription ko "Amanatdari Protocol" ke mutabiq verify karta hai. Expired ya ghalat dose ka koi imkan nahi chora jata.

Prescription Audit: Double-checking the drug name, dosage, and frequency against the physician's signed note.
Aseptic Hand-Wash: Strict WHO-standard hand hygiene protocol before touching any clinical supplies.
Sterile Field: Creating a "Clean Zone" at the patient's bedside for all IV components (Cannulas, Syringes, Fluids).

PHASE_02: DEPLOYMENT

PRECISION INSERTION

Hamara staff expert phlebotomists par mushtamil hai jo kam se kam dard ke saath cannula insert karte hain. Insertion ke baad drip ka flow rate doctor ki hidayat ke mutabiq set kiya jata hai.

Vein Mapping: Professional assessment to find the most stable and comfortable site for the cannula.
Flow Optimization: Manual or pump-based regulation to ensure medicine enters the bloodstream at the correct velocity.
Site Dressing: Using high-quality transparent dressings to monitor for any redness or swelling (Phlebitis check).
03

PHASE_03: CLINICAL_VIGILANCE

REACTION MONITORING

Infusion ke douran hamara staff patient ke vitals (BP, Pulse, Temperature) aur injection site ko musalsal monitor karta hai. Kisi bhi "Adverse Event" ki surat mein hamara protocol fori stop-and-report activation hai.

Anaphylaxis Watch: Immediate detection of allergic signs like itching, rashes, or breathing difficulty.
Site Integrity: Checking for infiltration (fluid leaking into tissue) or phlebitis (vein inflammation).
Emergency Reversal: Nursing staff is trained to stop infusion immediately and contact the primary physician if vitals fluctuate.
